About

I fell in love with coding almost 3 years ago during the COVID quarantine. My workplace was closed, and after spending the first day attempting to watch all 9 Fast & Furious movies, I decided to try to find something productive to do with my time. I had always said I wanted to learn "computer programming" but I didn't really have any idea what that meant. Luckily I stumbled across CS50 on EdX and was hooked immediately. By the time our quarantine ended 3 months later, I had spent hundreds of hours writing code, completed several MOOCs, and decided to change careers.

I'm currently wrapping up my Masters in Computing and Information Technology (MCIT) at Penn and working as a software engineer intern at SuChef where I'm working on migrating our code to React. I'm mostly interested in embedded software development, robotics, and the internet of things. I like to play around with my Raspberry Pi and Arduino, and I think it's really cool to write software that interacts with the physical world.

When I'm not writing code, I like to ski, cook, play chess, and practice Muay Thai and Brazilian Jiu Jitsu. I also love travelling with my wife - you can check out all the places we've been on my iTinerary page!
